#{msgs.Home_guidelines}

This guideline succeeds and supersedes the 2008 guideline, which was in use for drug approval and registration. It is prepared still with the same purpose: to provide manufacturers with information concerning documentation to be submitted for approval and registration of pharmaceutical products.

The guideline provides recommendation on the quality, safety and efficacy information for active pharmaceutical ingredient (API) and finished pharmaceutical product (FPP) that should be submitted to the Authority to support product dossiers (PD’s) for registration of medicines in Ethiopia.

The guideline applies to PDs containing an APIs of synthetic, semi-synthetic origin, biotechnological and an API that has been previously authorized through a finished product by a stringent regulatory authority and/or an API or its finished formulation official in the pharmacopeia. APIs from fermentation, biological, or herbal origin are covered by other guidelines. In situations where any of the guideline can’t address the documentation requirement of particular application the matter shall be resolved on a case by case basis in consultation with the Authority.
